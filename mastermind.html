JavaScript Mastermind game

by Daniele Sartirani

Custom difficulty
(N. of rows 1-12):
Length of code: 3 4
Number of colors: 4 5 6
Allow multiple use of the same color

HARD MODES:
Memory mode
Time run

How to play

With the mouse:Click on a color to pick it, then click on one of the bubbles to assign the color. As alternative, you can also drag and drop the color to the bubble.

With the keyboard: Press a number from 1 to 6 to assign the color to the bubble.
Press ENTER to submit the code,
Press ESC to abandon the game.
